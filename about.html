Dear Reader,

My name is Simon. I'm a games programmer from Germany living in Malmö, Sweden. I started out in Architecture and rigging game characters in Maya before moving over to the technical side of game development. In fact, my first contact with programming was creating rigging scripts for Maya, an area I still love to do. I have created a rigging plug-in for Maya, which is on my github page. Right now, I'm a programming student at FutureGames Malmö.

I believe in the design philosophy of Masahiro Sakurai, who was director of Super Smash Bros. The like to tell people this anecdote - I was researching the jump mechanic, so I was curious what Masahiro Sakurai has to say about it and he said "By the way, every time we start working on a new Smash Bros., I ask myself whether we even need a jump?"1 Now this is a funny perspective if you know the game Super Smash Bros. Now the gist of it is this - be mindful of the game mechanics your game is composed of.


Have a good day!
